Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, add your favourite items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email after placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and popular online payment services.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are usually processed within 1–2 business days. Shipping typically takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location.

Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order ships, you will receive an email with a tracking link so you can follow the delivery.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to many countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination.

Can I return or exchange a product?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us within 7 days of delivery, and we'll help arrange a replacement or refund.

How should I store coffee beans?

Store coffee beans in an airtight container in a cool, dry place, away from sunlight and moisture.

Who can I contact for help?

You can reach our support team through the Contact page or by email, and we'll respond as soon as possible.
